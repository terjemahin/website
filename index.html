Chapter 2400: Return

“Ah!” "Ah!"

Lei BA tiba-tiba memegangi kepalanya, matanya merah saat dia meraung, “” Tidak mungkin! Mengapa petir surgawi hanya menyambar anak saya dan bukan Anda? Pasti keluarga SU yang berencana membunuh anakku!” Lei BA suddenly clutched his head, his eyes red as he roared, “Impossible! Why did the heavenly lightning only strike my son and not you? It must be the SU family who plotted to kill my son!”

Lei BA tiba-tiba mengalihkan pandangannya ke arah Su Mo dan memelototinya. Lei BA suddenly turned his gaze towards su mo and glared at him.

Su mo sudah bertanya tentang masalah ini, dan dia buru-buru menangkupkan tinjunya dan berkata, “” Master sekte Lei, kamu salah paham. Ini seperti ini …” Su mo had already asked about the matter, and he hurriedly cupped his fists and said, “Sect master Lei, you’ve misunderstood. It’s like this …”

Dia segera menjelaskan alasannya dan menunjuk si bodoh kecil, ” “Itu semua karena si bodoh inilah anakmu tidak sengaja tersambar petir. Itu benar-benar tidak ada hubungannya dengan keluarga su-ku.” He immediately explained the situation and pointed at little fool, “It’s all because of this fool that your son was accidentally struck by lightning. It really has nothing to do with my su family.”

"Bodoh?" “A fool?”

Lei BA menatap tajam ke arah si bodoh kecil itu dan menunjuk ke arahnya, “” Kaulah yang membunuh anakku!” Lei BA glared fiercely a little lost and pointed at him, “You’re the one who killed my son!”
